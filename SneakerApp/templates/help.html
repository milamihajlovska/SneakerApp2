Shipping information


During the checkout process the customer is required to fill out the information for delivery including the home address, country and zipcode.

We offer free shipping for every placed order.

Standard delivery

  • Items arrive at the home address in 5 to 7 working days.

  • We do not deliver on weekends.

Returns


You can return orders purchased on SneakerApp.

You can still return products after 30 days as long as they are unworn.

You can also return and change products that are damaged or faulty.

All you need is the proof of purchase.
